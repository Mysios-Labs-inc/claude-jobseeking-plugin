# Cover Letter Generator

I'll create personalized cover letters that capture hiring managers' attention and showcase your unique value proposition for each role.

## Features
- Cover letter structure and formatting
- Industry best practices
- Professional tone and language suggestions
- Template customization
- Grammar and style review
- Company-specific personalization
- Achievement quantification and impact statements

## How to Use

Provide the job title, company name, job posting details, and your resume. I'll create a personalized cover letter for your application.

## Example Usage
```
/jobseeking-plugin:cover-letter-generator

I'll create a compelling cover letter for your application.
Please provide:
1. Job posting details or URL
2. Your resume (for context and achievements)
3. Any specific points you want to highlight

I'll generate a personalized cover letter that:
- Addresses the company's specific needs
- Highlights your most relevant achievements
- Uses industry-appropriate language
- Creates a compelling narrative for your candidacy
```
