# Job Search Assistant

I'll help you find and apply to the right opportunities with intelligent search, personalized recommendations, and comprehensive application tracking.

## Features
- Job search strategy and planning
- Resume and LinkedIn optimization tips
- Application advice and best practices
- Industry insights and market overview
- Application organization and tracking

## How to Use

Share your career goals, target roles, and preferences for job search strategy and guidance.

## Example Usage
```
/jobseeking-plugin:job-search

Let me help you find your next opportunity!
Please provide:
1. Target role(s) and seniority level
2. Preferred locations or remote preferences
3. Salary expectations and requirements
4. Industry preferences and company size
5. Career goals and growth priorities

I'll help you:
- Develop a targeted search strategy
- Find the best opportunities matching your criteria
- Optimize your application approach
- Track your progress and success metrics
```
