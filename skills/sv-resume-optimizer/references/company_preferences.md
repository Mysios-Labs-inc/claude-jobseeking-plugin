# Silicon Valley Company-Specific Resume Preferences

## Top Company Preferences (Based on 2026 Research)

### Google
**What they prioritize:**
- Embed stack keywords directly into experience bullets (recruiters use Boolean filters)
- Emphasize data structures, algorithms, and system design fundamentals
- Use X→Y→Z achievement format with quantified scale and cross-functional impact
- Technical depth with evidence of problem-solving capability

**Resume optimization:**
- Include algorithmic thinking examples
- Emphasize system design fundamentals
- Quantify scale: users served, data processed, system performance
- Show cross-functional collaboration impact

### Meta (Facebook)
**What they prioritize:**
- Highlight open-source contributions with repo links and impact descriptions
- Focus on measurable outcomes with concise, skill-focused summaries
- Demonstrate system design experience and technical leadership
- Evidence of building products that scale to millions of users

**Resume optimization:**
- Add GitHub contribution graphs/statistics
- Emphasize technical leadership and mentorship
- Show system architecture and scalability experience
- Include metrics around user growth and engagement

### Apple
**What they prioritize:**
- Emphasize system-level fundamentals, especially for hardware roles
- Show deep technical competency with evidence of architectural thinking
- Prepare for technical depth evaluation in later interview stages
- Focus on end-to-end user experience and product quality

**Resume optimization:**
- Focus on system-level thinking and architectural decisions
- Emphasize performance optimization and hardware considerations
- Show attention to user experience and product quality
- Include any hardware/firmware experience

### Netflix
**What they prioritize:**
- Connect technical work to product/business metrics (user engagement, performance)
- Translate engineering improvements into business impact (hours-watched, latency, churn)
- Focus on data-driven decision making and product orientation
- Demonstrate experience with real-time, high-throughput systems

**Resume optimization:**
- Connect all technical work to business metrics
- Emphasize real-time system design and performance
- Show data-driven decision making
- Include metrics around user engagement and business impact

### Uber
**What they prioritize:**
- Demonstrate end-to-end ownership of real-time, high-throughput systems
- Quantify scope: users served, latency improvements, throughput metrics
- Show experience with distributed systems and reliability engineering
- Evidence of building systems that handle massive scale

**Resume optimization:**
- Emphasize distributed systems experience
- Show end-to-end ownership and operational responsibility
- Include scale metrics: throughput, latency, availability
- Demonstrate reliability engineering practices

### Airbnb
**What they prioritize:**
- Emphasize product impact, cross-functional collaboration
- Show measurable outcomes and user-focused engineering decisions
- Demonstrate experience scaling consumer-facing applications
- Evidence of building delightful user experiences

**Resume optimization:**
- Focus on product impact and user experience
- Show cross-functional collaboration skills
- Emphasize growth and engagement metrics
- Include consumer-facing application experience

## General Silicon Valley Preferences

### Technical Excellence
- Demonstrate deep technical competency
- Show evidence of solving complex engineering problems
- Include performance optimization and scalability examples
- Emphasize clean code and engineering best practices

### Product Mindset
- Connect technical work to user/business outcomes
- Show understanding of product development lifecycle
- Demonstrate ability to make data-driven decisions
- Include examples of feature impact and user engagement

### Scale and Performance
- Quantify system scale: users, requests, data volume
- Include performance improvements with specific metrics
- Show experience with high-availability systems
- Demonstrate understanding of distributed systems

### Innovation and Growth
- Include examples of technical innovation
- Show ability to ship products and features quickly
- Demonstrate experience with A/B testing and experimentation
- Evidence of contributing to technical strategy and roadmap
